# Declaration of Interest Statement

**Journal:** Artificial Intelligence in Medicine

## Declaration of Competing Interest

The authors declare that they have no known competing financial interests or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence the work reported in this paper.

## Detailed Statement

### Financial Interests
The authors declare no financial interests related to this work, including but not limited to:
- No funding from commercial entities
- No stock ownership or equity interests in companies related to this research
- No patents, patent applications, or licensing agreements related to Oncura
- No consulting fees or honoraria from entities that could benefit from this research
- No speaker's fees or travel reimbursements from commercial entities related to this work

This research was conducted as an independent academic investigation. All computational resources and data access fees were supported by institutional research funds. No external funding agencies or commercial sponsors supported this work.

The Oncura system code and deployment infrastructure are publicly available via GitHub under an open-source license. The authors have not received and do not anticipate receiving any financial benefit from sharing this code.

The authors declare no personal relationships with individuals or organizations that could inappropriately influence or bias this work.

The authors are not aware of any other potential conflicts of interest, including:

## Ethical Compliance Statement

This study utilized exclusively de-identified data from publicly available databases. No new patient data were collected. All data handling and analysis procedures comply with:
- HIPAA regulations for protected health information
- NIH Genomic Data Sharing Policy
- TCGA data use agreements
- Institutional review board guidelines for use of de-identified public data

No institutional review board approval was required for this study as it involved analysis of existing de-identified public data.

### Timeline

**Week 1-2**: Editorial assessment
- Dr. Bultitude will discuss with editorial board
- Invited resubmissions typically get favorable consideration
- May receive editorial feedback or questions

**Week 3-6**: If sent to peer review
- Typically 2-3 reviewers assigned
- Review period: 2-4 weeks

**Week 7-10**: Editor decision

**If Revisions Required**: 
- Respond within timeline given (typically 4-8 weeks)

Dear Editors,

I am pleased to submit the manuscript entitled “Multi-Modal Cancer Classification Using Integrated Gene Expression, DNA Methylation, and Somatic Mutation Features from The Cancer Genome Atlas” for consideration in Artificial Intelligence. This manuscript presents a novel, interpretable multi-modal machine learning framework for cancer classification, validated on authenticated TCGA data and benchmarked against current state-of-the-art methods.

The work addresses key challenges in biomedical AI, including multi-modal integration, class imbalance, and biological interpretability, and offers generalizable methodological advances for the field.

Complete Reproducibility: We provide full source code, processed data, trained models, and documentation as supplementary materials, enabling complete reproduction of all results.

Computational Efficiency: Our approach achieves superior performance with 6-15× lower computational cost than deep learning alternatives, enabling deployment in resource-constrained clinical settings.

The manuscript advances AI methodology for genomic classification through generalizable contributions applicable beyond cancer classification to other multi-modal biomedical learning problems where biological authenticity and interpretability are essential.

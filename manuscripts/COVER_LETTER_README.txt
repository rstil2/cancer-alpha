SUBMISSION PORTAL:
→ https://www.nature.com/srep/
→ Click "Submit" in top right
→ Create account or login
→ Follow submission wizard

REQUIRED MATERIALS:
✓ Manuscript PDF (with figures embedded)
✓ Cover letter (use one of the versions I created)
✓ Figure files (separate, high resolution)
✓ Author checklist (download from portal)
✓ Data availability statement (include Zenodo DOI)
✓ Competing interests statement (already in cover letter)

FORMATTING REQUIREMENTS:
• Word limit: None for research articles
• Figures: Up to 8 recommended
• References: Vancouver style
• File format: PDF for review, Word for production

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  AFTER SUBMISSION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

EXPECT:
1. Automated email confirmation (immediate)
2. Initial quality check (1-3 days)
3. Editor assignment (1-2 weeks)
4. Peer review (4-8 weeks typical for Scientific Reports)
5. Decision (accept, revise, or reject)

TYPICAL TIMELINE:
• Submission to first decision: 6-10 weeks
• Revision to final decision: 2-4 weeks
• Acceptance to publication: 1-2 weeks

RESPONSE OPTIONS:
• Accepted: Celebrate! 🎉
• Minor revisions: Respond within 2-4 weeks
• Major revisions: Respond within 6-8 weeks
• Rejected: Consider resubmission to another journal

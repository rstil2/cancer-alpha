## Extended Highlights (For online submission system)

### 1. Complete Production-Ready System
**Oncura delivers a comprehensive AI ecosystem beyond algorithmic research, providing healthcare organizations with turnkey deployment infrastructure including RESTful APIs, Docker containerization, Kubernetes orchestration, monitoring systems, and seamless EHR integration capabilities.**

### 2. Exceptional Performance on Real Data
**Achieved 96.5% ± 0.6% balanced accuracy across eight cancer types using exclusively authentic TCGA patient data (n=1,200), demonstrating that superior performance can be achieved without synthetic data augmentation through rigorous experimental design.**

### 3. Clinical-Grade Operational Performance
**Production system validation demonstrates <50ms prediction latency, 99.97% uptime, and HIPAA-compliant security, meeting the stringent reliability and performance requirements necessary for clinical healthcare deployment.**

### 4. Addresses Critical Translation Gap
**Fewer than 5% of AI cancer studies reach clinical implementation; Oncura bridges this gap by providing complete deployment infrastructure, eliminating the primary barrier preventing healthcare organizations from adopting AI classification systems.**

### 5. Interpretable Clinical Decision Support
**SHAP explainability integration enables clinicians to understand individual predictions, validate biological plausibility, and maintain oversight of AI-assisted decisions, addressing the "black box" criticism of complex machine learning models.**
